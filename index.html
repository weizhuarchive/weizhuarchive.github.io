Relief Package

High Tech Farm Laboratory

Architecture is not a land destroyer, but here, it serves as a land restoration machine. It fix the problem of declining groundwater levels caused by excessive water usage in agriculture and drought climate. The building functions as a water collector and reservoir, gathering rainwater, stormwater, and any other surface water from nature to supply the building and farm at different stages of cycles.
