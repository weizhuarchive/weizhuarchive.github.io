Stack & Array

The cycle of stack and array formation is based on the water usage of the crops and the building. As farming season begin, the water demand for crop planting increases , the water stored in the container will largely flow to the farmland. At the same time, to maintain water balance between the ground and building, the water supply for the building will be reduced. This reduction prompts the pieces to stack together, forming a self-water circulation system using the wastewater produced by the pieces themselves. When enter fallow, the pieces go back to array and connect to water tank to charge water again.
