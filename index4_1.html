• STUDENT DORMITORY

      The student dormitory, nestled amidst nature, caters to farm study participants with 12 single-bed and 10 double-bed rooms. A beacon of sustainability, its rooftop doubles as a rainwater collector, aligned with the ethos of utilizing nature's resources. Water sourced from nature and recycled origins sustains the dormitory. Its circular design envelopes private spaces, while an inner garden fosters social connections. Each room offers sweeping views of surrounding farmland, epitomizing harmony between academia and nature. This dormitory weaves sustainable living, architectural innovation, and educational growth into a tapestry that nurtures both students and the environment.
